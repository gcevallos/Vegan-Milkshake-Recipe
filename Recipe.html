Vegan Chocolate
Moonshake

Looking for your chocolate fix? Look no further! This chocolate milkshake is perfectly creamy, chocolatey, and completely dairy free.

  • Prep: 5 minutes
  • Total Time: 5 minutes
  • Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 4 cups chocolate almond milk
  • 4 pitted dates

Instructions

  1. Mash banana and avocado.
  2. Place all ingrdients in blender and blend until smooth. Test sweetness and add natural sweetner if desired.
  3. Serve immediately.
